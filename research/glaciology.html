Research Topic

Glaciology

Glaciers and ice sheets are sensitive indicators of climate change and major contributors to global sea-level rise. My glaciology work focuses on the dynamics of ice flow, subglacial hydrology, and the interactions between glaciers and the surrounding landscape.

Ice Dynamics Subglacial Hydrology Mass Balance Climate Change Remote Sensing
Motivation

Key research questions

  • How do subglacial drainage systems modulate ice velocity and contribute to glacier instability?
  • What is the role of basal sliding versus internal deformation in controlling ice sheet dynamics?
  • How will continued warming affect the timing and magnitude of glacier-fed river discharge?
Approach

Methods & data

I combine satellite-derived velocity fields, airborne radar ice-thickness measurements, and numerical ice-flow models to investigate glacier dynamics across a range of climatic and topographic settings.
